[08] HOUSTON, TX · EST. 1962 ABOUT

From a Houston machine shop
to Africa to the Arctic.

Stark Industries was founded in 1962 as a machine shop in Houston, Texas by Jess Stark. As the business grew, Jess steered Stark Industries into manufacturing specialized high pressure compressor packages in 1969.

Today, Stark designs, maintains, and re-manufactures all compressors in our own 40,000 square foot facility on 1¼ acres in the heart of Houston.

Our goal: always minimum space & weight, combined with maximum efficiency.

You will find Stark compressors from Africa to the Arctic Circle. Our compressors are built to withstand the harshest of environments, using stainless-steel fittings whenever possible.

"Stark Industries has consistently stayed away from programmable controllers on our compressors. The conventional controls on all of our equipment manufactured since 1969 have been extremely reliable and are still running today. This is in stark contrast to the enormous failure rate of PLCs. Simplicity translates directly to reliability & long life." — STARK INDUSTRIES PHILOSOPHY
